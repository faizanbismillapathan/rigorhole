Micro Gun Drilling Machine

A Micro Gun Drilling Machine is a specialized machining equipment used for drilling small diameter holes with high precision and accuracy, commonly used in industries such as medical.

BTA

BTA stands for Boring and trepanning Association. It comprises of a special hollow tooling that allows pressurized coolant to flow into the hole on the outside of the drill, and evacuates chips through an internal hole in the drill. BTA drilling can produce deep holes typically from dia 20mm or greater and can reach L/D of 400:1.

3-Axis Machine

A 3-Axis Machine is a machining tool capable of performing operations in three directions: X-axis, Y-axis, and Z-axis. It enables precise and controlled movements for milling, drilling, and cutting processes.
