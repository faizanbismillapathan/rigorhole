Welcome to RigorHole

We provide services more than 25 years

RigorHole was established in 2022 in Bhiwandi, India's Machine manufacturing center. We specialize in manufacturing BTA, 3-Axis machine, 2-Axis machine, Single Axis machine. Our co-founder has 25 years of experience in machine manufacturing, Retrofitting and Machine reconditioning.

It is our policy to supply service which meet or exceed both the design requirement and quality specifications of customer. Our machines are most suited in Defence, Oil and Gas, Automotive, Aerospace, Medical Implants, Die and Mould etc.

We pride ourselves in the ability to truly offer a world class solution and guarantee performance and productivity over the life of the product. We offer solutions for Gun drilling, BTA (STS) / Ejector drilling, Pull Boring, Trepanning, Counter boring. We also provide technical services to customer to upgrade/enhance machines to increase the efficiency in terms of quality and productivity.

Our Mission And Vision

Empowering industries with precision machines and exceptional services, we envision being a global leader in manufacturing, retrofitting, and enhancing machines for enhanced quality and productivity.
